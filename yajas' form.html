Programmes Offered

BCA

The BCA programme is oriented toward building a brilliant career for Information Technology professionals. The programme is focused on building application based skills which include the creative and logical thinking skills. SICSR provides professionals seeking a smart career in the IT area with an evaluation that meets international requirements. This degree equips students with the skills to evaluate, design, and develop software systems. Students will eventually comprehend logic development ideas and industry best software practices.

BBA(IT)

The BBA (IT) programme is focused on the development of graduate traits such as technology implementation, innovation, entrepreneurship, ethics, and human values for global citizens. Our students are always ready to learn about new technology emerging in the market through some of the BBA in IT colleges in India. SICSR being one of the top 10 BBA (IT) institutes in India, offers students seeking a smart career in the IT field in BBA that conforms with national and international standards. This degree prepares students to deliver techno-management solutions to challenges in the corporate, social, and government sectors by teaching them communication skills as well as creative thinking.

Msc(CA)

MSc in Computer Applications is the perfect blend of Technology implementation, application design, Innovation, Security, Entrepreneurship, and Ethics for the development of global citizens.

MBA(IT)

The MBA (IT) programme at SICSR offers students a perfect blend of technology and management courses. This programme comprises five specialisations Banking and Finance, Data Analytics, IT Infrastructure Management, Marketing and Software Project Management. This degree prepares students to take up roles such as Business Analyst, IT Management Consultant, IT Architect, Data Processing Manager, Systems Software Manager and Product Manager, to name a few.
